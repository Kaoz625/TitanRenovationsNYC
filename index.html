What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive drywall and interior renovation services for NYC residences and commercial spaces.

🧱

Drywall Installation

Full-scope drywall from framing to flawless finish. We handle both residential and commercial projects.

  • Steel & wood framing
  • Drywall hanging & screwing
  • Taping, mudding & feathering
  • Sanding (Level 4 & 5 finish)
  • Dust control & containment
🏠

Interior Drywall & Partitions

Full-scope interior drywall for residential and commercial spaces — new builds, gut-outs, and room reconfigurations.

  • New partition walls
  • Room additions & reconfiguration
  • Basement drywall finishing
  • Closet & built-in framing
  • NYC code-compliant assemblies
🔧

Repair & Restoration

Fast, expert repair work for water damage, impact holes, and aged or failing drywall systems.

  • Water & mold damage repair
  • Impact hole patching
  • Full skim coat application
  • Texture matching
  • Paint-ready surface prep
🏢

Commercial Buildout

Retail spaces, offices, and commercial interiors — on schedule, within code, built for daily use.

  • Drop ceiling & grid systems
  • Office partition walls
  • Retail & restaurant buildout
  • Fire-rated assemblies
  • Flexible scheduling for tenants
🎨

Finishing & Priming

We take walls to paint-ready — tight seams, smooth surfaces, and clean edges every time.

  • Level 4 and Level 5 finishing
  • Primer coat application
  • Architectural detail finishing
  • Curved & arched walls
  • Pre-paint inspection included
📐

Estimates & Consulting

  • Free on-site estimates
  • Scope & materials planning
  • Budget range guidance
  • No pressure, no obligation
  • Fast turnaround on quotes
